# Tweet Templates & Hooks

Use these patterns to kickstart the drafting process.

## 1. Curiosity Hooks
- "I've spent 10 hours researching [Topic] so you don't have to. Here's what I found:"
- "90% of people get [Topic] wrong. Here is the 10% truth that changes everything:"
- "The hidden secret to [Benefit] isn't [Common Guess]. It's [Surprising Truth]."

## 2. Listicles (The Value Bomb)
- "5 tools that will save you 10+ hours a week on [Task]:"
- "I analyzed the top 10 [Niche] accounts. Here are 3 things they all do:"
- "Stop [Bad Habit]. Start [Good Habit]. Here is how:"

## 3. The Contrarian (The Hot Take)
- "Unpopular opinion: [Bold Statement]."
- "Everyone is talking about [Trend], but everyone is missing [Key Detail]."
